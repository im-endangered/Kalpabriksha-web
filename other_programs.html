History: Gaze at the Maze
History: Gaze at the Maze is an online history workshop on Nepalese history, starting from 3000 years back, targeted to school students. The goal of this session lies in uniting Nepali youths with their ancestors, culture, and in signifying their importance. Kalpabriksha has successfully completed two editions of this session, and is preparing to launch the third edition soon.

Yuwantar
Yuwantar is an annual carnival event of Kalpabriksha, the first edition of which was held on December 31, 2022. The event features special talks about mental health, interactions with several organizations working for the society, fun activities (singing, dancing, painting) to engage, good food to enjoy, and a sense of togetherness. The main motive of the carnival is to synchronize visionary voices on the sensitive issue of emotional well-being.
